Networking Basics

Key Principles

  • IP address – Unique address of a device.
  • Subnet Mask – Divides network and hosts.
  • Router – Connects different networks.
  • Switch – Connect devices on a network.
  • Protocol –Rules for communication(HTTP, TCP/IP)

Why Networking is Important

Networking is important because it connects devices, enables communication, shares resources, and supports security and modern technologies. Without networking, computers would work only in isolation.
